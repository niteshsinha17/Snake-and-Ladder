Snake and Ladder Web

We all had played Snake and Ladder on the backside of the notebook during our childhood. But now the world has been changed. Everything is now online which we can enjoy and we the engineers want to implement the same. In this Game I tried to do the same. Let's bring our childhood memory back.

Playing Options:

1. You can play with Computer

2.You can play with your friend by sitting together


Online Multiplayer Option is comming soon

Rules:

1. Player can start moving after getting 6 for one time.

2. When player get 6 first time then that 6 is not counted in steps.

3. Player who will reaches to 100 first will wins.

4. Don't refesh the website while playing.

5. There is no limit of getting 6.
